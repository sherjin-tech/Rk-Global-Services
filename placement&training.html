Placement Process

At RK Educations, we are committed to not just training but also ensuring our candidates secure the right job. Our 100% placement guarantee sets us apart, reflecting our confidence in the quality of our training and our dedicated placement support.

1. Training Completion :

Candidates undergo industry-specific training.

2. Mock Interviews and Resume Building :

Preparing candidates for the job market.

3. Job Matching and Placement :

We connect candidates with suitable employers.

4. Job Offer and Acceptance :

Candidates receive offers and start their new roles.

Our Training Programs

We offer specialized training programs to meet the needs of various industries, ensuring our candidates gain the skills and knowledge required to excel in their careers. Our programs are designed by industry experts, focusing on both technical and soft skills to create well-rounded professionals.

IT Training

Courses include software development, programming languages, web development, data analytics, and more.

Non-IT Training

Programs covering project management, business analysis, sales and marketing, and customer service.

Banking Training

Prepare for roles in retail banking, investment banking, financial analysis, risk management, and compliance.

Manufacturing Training

Focused on production management, quality assurance, supply chain management, and maintenance operations.

All our programs include live projects, case studies, and hands-on experience to build job-ready skills.
